About

Jake Reichenauer is a fashion and commercial photographer based in New York City. Working in both analog and digital formats, his process emphasizes clean execution and refined color.

He has trained in darkroom printing under master printer Jorge Ochoa, and studied advanced color and retouching with Natalia Taffarel. His work often favors quiet framing, natural gestures, and a subtle interplay of tone and light.

Styles include studio lookbooks, lifestyle campaigns, bodywear, and model development. He is currently available for editorial, commercial, and collaborative work.
